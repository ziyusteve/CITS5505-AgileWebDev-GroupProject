About Data Sharing
When you share a dataset, the recipient will be able to view your data and the analysis results, but they won't be able to modify or delete your original data.

  • Collaborate with team members on data analysis
  • Share insights with stakeholders and decision makers
  • Get feedback on your data interpretations
  • Maintain control over who can access your data

You maintain full control over your data:

  • Choose exactly who can access your datasets
  • Revoke access at any time
  • Track who has access to your data
  • Recipients cannot share your data with others
